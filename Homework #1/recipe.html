Below is a simple recipe for making pancakes

# Explain the purpose of the site

What are the ingredients that we are going to use:

# Start with the ingrediends
  • 100 g of plain four
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300 ml of milk
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sunflower or vegetable oil
  • lemon wedges to serve (as prefered)
  • granulated sugar to serve (as prefered)

Now the steps you need to take to complete the recipe and enjoy your pancakes

  1. Put 100g of plain flour, 300ml of milk, 2 large eggs, 1 tablespoon of sunflower and a pinch of salt into a large bowl and whisk it to a smooth batter.
  2. Either wait for 30 minutes for it to rest or you can start cooking right away if you dont have time.
  3. Set a medium frying pan or a crepe pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it out with some oiled kitchen paper.
  4. When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min each side until golden, keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges and granulated sugar, or your favourite filling
